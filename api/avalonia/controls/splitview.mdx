### UseLightDismissOverlayMode

Gets or sets whether WinUI equivalent LightDismissOverlayMode is enabled

When enabled, and the pane is open in Overlay or CompactOverlay mode,
the contents of the [Avalonia.Controls.SplitView](xref:Avalonia.Controls.SplitView) are darkened to visually separate the open pane
and the rest of the [Avalonia.Controls.SplitView](xref:Avalonia.Controls.SplitView).

```csharp
public System.Boolean UseLightDismissOverlayMode { get; set; }
```
