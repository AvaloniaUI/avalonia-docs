The official Avalonia documentation is written and maintained in English. However, Avalonia welcomes community efforts translating the docs into other languages. This page collects links to community translations, so you can look for documentation in your preferred language.

These translations are **unofficial** and are not maintained by the Avalonia team. They may be incomplete, out of date, or differ from the current English documentation. For the most accurate and up-to-date information, always refer to the official English documentation on this website.

## Contributing a translation

Have you translated part of the Avalonia documentation, or maintain a translation that isn't listed here? We'd love to include it.

To get your translation added to this page, please start a thread in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/AvaloniaUI/avalonia-docs/discussions).
