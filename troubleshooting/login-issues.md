This page troubleshoots common issues encountered when logging into an [Avalonia Plus, Pro or Enterprise](https://avaloniaui.net/pricing), or [XPF](/xpf) subscription.

## Incorrect credentials

Your Avalonia credentials are used to sign in to the [portal](https://portal.avaloniaui.net/), as well as the Avalonia for Visual Studio Extension.

The email associated with your account is usually the work email provided by your organization.

If you have forgotten your password, try [resetting it](https://portal.avaloniaui.net/forgot-password).

## Never received an invitation email

Check your junk/spam folder, in case your email client filtered the email there.

## Error message: "No active subscription seat found."

Your organization has a subscription, but no seat has been assigned to you. Please contact your organization's Admin to request seat assignment. Instructions are available on the [Assigning seats](/tools/assigning-seats) page.
