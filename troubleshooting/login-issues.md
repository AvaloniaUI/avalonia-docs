This page troubleshoots common issues encountered when logging into an [Avalonia Plus, Pro, Enterprise](https://avaloniaui.net/pricing), or [XPF](/xpf) subscription.

## Incorrect credentials

Your Avalonia credentials are used to sign in to the [portal](https://portal.avaloniaui.net/). These are the same credentials you use to access Avalonia development tools, such as the [Avalonia for Visual Studio extension](/tools/visual-studio-extension), [Dev Tools](/tools/developer-tools/installation), or [Parcel](/tools/parcel/setup).

The email associated with your account is usually the work email provided by your organization.

If you have forgotten your password, try [resetting it](https://portal.avaloniaui.net/forgot-password).

## Never received an invitation email

Check your junk or spam folder, in case your email client filtered the message there.

Consider adding `@avaloniaui.net` to your safe senders list, to avoid missing emails from us.

## Error message: "No active subscription seat found."

Your organization has a subscription, but no seat has been assigned to you. Please contact your organization's Admin to request seat assignment. Instructions are available on the [Assigning seats](/tools/assigning-seats) page.

## I need more help

More information is available at the [portal](https://portal.avaloniaui.net/).
